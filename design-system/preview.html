Motion · easing curves6 easings · 5 durations

Each card animates on loop · same 1500ms duration · different easing. Notice how easeOutExpo (default) settles fast and gentle · easeOutBack overshoots slightly · easeOutBounce is playful (use sparingly) · linear is for parallax only.
